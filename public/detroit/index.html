Sustainable community homes in Detroit

We are a cooperative investment project devoted to building and stewarding affordable co-living in Detroit. We develop housing that is ecological, permanently affordable, and moving towards community governance. We are proud to be part of Detroit's inevitable rebuilding and resilience.

Building our values

Cultivating community

We believe humans are happiest when they feel safe and abundant in sharing what they have and can trust and depend on their neighbors. Through regular community meals and meal preps, having a shared neighborhood text chat.

Affordability

Affordable housing is the foundation of a strong community. We are building a community land trust that will allow local families and residents to stay and benefit from improvements in their neighborhood.

Sustainability

Detroit Arcology aims to design, experiment with, and share best practices for living sustainably in a way that benefits and respects the long-term health of the local and global environment and its people.
