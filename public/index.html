Arcology

sustainable community homes

We are a community of entrepreneurs, artists, hackers, researchers, family members, and neighbors building sustainable homes together. We mesh with rooftop internets, throw music nights, create technology, generate solar power, raise honeybees, and cultivate community homes (formerly in Seattle and NYC, currently in Detroit).

Our goal is a network of cooperative, inter-generational landships that nurture tribes of happy humans.
