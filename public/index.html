Arcology

A community home for creating and learning.

Live, play, and work with a community of entrepreneurs, artists, musicians, hackers, makers, crafters, scientists, researchers, and engineers. Named after Paolo Soleri's integrated architecture and ecology at Arcosanti, Arcology draws inspiration from a magical purple nerd castle at MIT, Black Rock City, Woodard Lane Cohousing, the Israeli moshav at Nahalal, the Buddhist community home Marpa House, and the great work from SimCity.

An inter-generational landship to cultivate tribes of happy humans.

Timeline
  • Phase One (completed) 15 June 2016 - 31 August 2016 Minimum liveable product in Bushwick, one year prototype. 2 bunks in a shared room, 1 private room. Workspace for two. Internet-controlled door buzzer.
  • Phase Two (imminent) 1 September 2017 - 31 August 2018 Rented loft space in Dumbo, one year transitional space. 4 bunk beds, 2 private rooms. Workspace for 4. Door locks, rent payments, expenses and house governed on Ethereum smart contract. Rooftop garden and apiary.
  • Phase Three (landship) 1 September 2018 - 31 August 2019 L train closes. 4-story building, 10 bunks in shared rooms, 4 private rooms, 1 family apartment. Workspace for 10. Food co-op, coffeeshop, school, event space on ground floor. Equity and dividends in the arcology paid on Ethereum.
  • Phase Four (self-governance) 1 September 2019 - 31 August 2020 Building achieves energy sustainability and self-governance with a system for training the next generation of leaders.
