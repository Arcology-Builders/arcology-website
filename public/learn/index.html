Arcology Learning

Our work at Arcology Builders is supported by our educational mission, which is to help students learn computer science and computer engineering, become more self-reliant, experience confidence and success, and have fun while doing it.

Our Methods

  • We accept students for an entire quarter or for a major class project.
  • We teach through screensharing and remote desktop. All work and typing is done on the student's computer through the student's fingertips, to help solidify concepts through practice and tactile feedback.
  • We practice pair programming and live coding.
