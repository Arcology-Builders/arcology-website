<h1>Staking and Governance for Arcology</h1> # <span class="header">Staking and Shares</span> This section is aspirational and has not been implemented yet in house software. It reflects a tentative direction of Arcology as a cooperative home, and may change without warning. In future subleases, this section will become increasingly detailed and replace words of the form "should" with words of the form "is" or "does". Residents of Arcology can earn stake (shares) in the home, which gives them a right to earn dividends from house profits and conversely participate in risks of default on the master lease. Profits from the home may be distributed proportionally to residents holding shares (shareholders) at periodic intervals. Some shares hold a fix dividend rate and should be paid first Any Deposit Payment that is held by Arcology should be applied to the deposit of the master lease. If the Master Deposit is defaulted for lack of paying the Master Rent or damage to the property that has not been repaired by the Master Move-out Date, the Deposit Amounts of long-term residents at the Move-Out Date will experience a corresponding loss.
